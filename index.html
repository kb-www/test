Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking, walk-ins, and finding us.

How can I book an appointment at Killiney Barbers?
Use our online booking at book.killineybarbers.ie to pick a time and service in a few taps, call (01) 513 6000, or walk in during opening hours—we welcome walk-ins and will fit you in when we can; booking ahead is best for busy times.
What is the new Killiney Barbers booking experience?

Don’t just book a time—book a team.

Our new, secure booking system is built for speed: no accounts, no passwords, and no hassle. Just pick your services, and we’ll coordinate our staff so your whole group starts together.

Stay in the loop with instant WhatsApp reminders. No more sitting on the bench—get in, get finished, and get back to your day.

Do you take walk-ins at Killiney Barbers?
Yes. Walk-ins are welcome during our opening hours. We recommend booking online or by phone if you want a specific time, especially at peak times.
Where is Killiney Barbers located?
We are located at 2B Killiney Shopping Centre, Rochestown Avenue, Dun Laoghaire, Co. Dublin, A96 A528
